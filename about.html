“PRAYAN FOUNDATION” is a non-government organization registered under NITI AAYOG, which has been founded by a group of sedulous undergraduate students who aim to be the change that they aspire to see in the world. We thoroughly believe that “Life isn’t lived, till its lived for others” and following the same mantra we march forward each day with love, care and kindness instilled in our hearts.
Creating maximum number of full fledged working projects along with individual units in other colleges , with highly motivated volunteers.
