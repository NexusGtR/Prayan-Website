"Education is a right, not a privilege: Join us to make it accessible to all children"

"Sambhav" is the initiative by students of Miranda House College. As the name suggests, 'anything is possible' or 'could be achieved' if we work together. The social cause we are targeting is "Child Education" to ensure basic and elementary education to children living in slum areas. The unit comprises of a team of nearly 25 members comprising the head, co-head, joint secretary/treasurer along with the volunteers. The unit has recently started to provide basic education to underprivileged children who have never had the opportunity to attend school. Education is essential for one's life and future, and we aims to give these children the chance to learn and build their knowledge, skills, and abilities.

"Health And Hygiene – Better than wealth and digene!"

Proiect Swashrit recognizes the importance of good health as the greatest fortune. With this in mind, the project strives to improve the well-being of individuals through various initiatives such as raising awareness about health, distributing medicines and hygiene products, and conducting workshops on various health-related topics. By catering to people of all age groups, Swashrit has impacted over 1000+ lives, donating useful daily essentials such as nail cutters, combs, earthen pots, and refreshments to maintain personal hygiene at old age homes. Swashrit believes that evolution is an inherent part of life, and as such, it is essential to expand one's horizons to adapt to changing times. This is why the organization has taken on several initiatives to raise awareness about critical health issues, including women's hygiene, sexually transmitted diseases (STDs), and mental health. While these areas pose significant challenges, Swashrit is committed to overcoming them and improving the lives of those it serves.

"Thinking is easy, acting is difficult, and to put one's thoughts into action is the most difficult thing in the world."

Svakarman" is the initiative by students of Daulat Ram College. "svakarman" is a Sanskrit term which means self employment, we aim to empower individuals and community by providing them with the resources knowledge and the support that they need and run successful businesses day by contributing to local economic growth and development . It involves exploring skills of people living in slums and monetizing it so that these people can earn a good profit .Every skill has a market and some kind of demand, some are more in demand and some in less but that does not change the fact that there is a demand for almost every skill that exists i.e. sewing, painting, embroidering, cooking, art & craft, etc. The list is endless and so are the opportunities. We are a team of 29 zealous members consisting of a joint secretary ,head and volunteers and we believe that true charity is not giving bread or money but by providing employment.

Animal protection is education to humanity

The initiative is named Karuna keeping in mind the objective of SGTB Khalsa College. Karuna is a Sanskrit term which means compassion. We at Karuna Extend that empathy, compassion and kindness to all other animals, including animals raised and killed for food, clothing, or other such uses. Karuna works with the aim of Jeevarakshanam samvardhan which means "fostering and rearing animals for their survival". Our motto is to provide a protected environment for animals. At our Khalsa unit, we aim to give a protected environment to the animals. Our initiative "Karuna" is an illustration of that. At Karuna, we aspire to create a small protected world for these to co-exist happily. This idea focuses on promoting a holistic approach to animal welfare, which encompasses not only the physical health and safety of animals, but also their emotional well-being, socialization, and mental enrichment. Our team of dedicated members ensures that animals get compassionate care and love. We strive to create a community that values the presence of these innocent beings.
